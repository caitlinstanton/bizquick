Small Business Ideas

Create a Business Plan

Consider your target audience, how you plan on financing your business, marketing and advertising, legal structure, your business model, and your competition. Create a name for your business that represents whatever product or service you offer, and make sure it is not already trademarked.

How to Make a Business Plan

Financing

Talk with your bank, get local investors, seek venture capitalists or angel investors, use crowd funding, and approach family and friends. In order to get your business off the ground, research and apply for necessary permits and licenses. Be knowledgeable of local and federal tax and insurance requirements. You may want to seek professional legal advice or do further research.
